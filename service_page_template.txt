SECTION 1 (HERO):
- H1: [Service Name] Services
- Subheadline: One clear benefit statement (results-focused)
- 2 CTAs: Primary (Get Started/Consultation), Secondary (Pricing/Portfolio)
- 3 Key highlights below CTAs

SECTION 2 (PROBLEM):
- Headline: Question format ("Are you facing...")
- 3-4 pain points in card format
- Each pain point = common challenge your target customer has
- Empathy statement at bottom

SECTION 3 (SOLUTION):
- Headline: "Our [Service] Solutions"
- 2-4 solution types/packages/approaches
- Features in bullet points
- CTA on each card if appropriate

SECTION 4 (PROCESS):
- Headline: "How We Deliver [Service]"
- 4-6 steps (numbered)
- Brief description per step
- Visual flow (arrows between steps)

SECTION 5 (BENEFITS):
- Headline: "Why Choose Our Services"
- 6 key benefits (not features!)
- Icon + title + 1-2 sentence description
- Focus on outcomes

SECTION 6 (PROOF):
- Headline: "Real Results for Real Businesses"
- 2-3 case studies
- Specific metrics and numbers
- Optional: Client testimonial quote

SECTION 7 (TECH):
- Only for technical services
- Show logos of technologies/platforms
- Organize by category if many

SECTION 8 (FAQ):
- 4-6 most common questions
- Accordion/expandable format
- Answers should be helpful, not salesy

SECTION 9 (CTA):
- Headline: Action-oriented
- Subheadline: What happens next
- Single prominent CTA button
- Contact info visible
